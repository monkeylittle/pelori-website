Pelori — the bits a group ride actually needs

Pelori is a group-cycling app that handles the four things you
message friends about before every ride: plan it, see who's coming,
keep the chat in one place, and find new rides nearby.

This is the open beta. It's the standard tier — everything you need
to plan and run a recurring group ride:

— Plan a ride. Date, time, meeting pin, recurring or one-off. "Every
  Tuesday at 7pm" is one tap. Invite your crew and the ride lives
  somewhere everyone can find.
— RSVP per occurrence. Going, not going, or maybe. Resets every week
  so the regulars don't have to rebook the same ride.
— One thread per ride. Group chat right next to the ride it belongs
  to. Replaces the five overlapping group chats you've got now.
— Discover. Public rides from cycling clubs, bike shops, and weekend
  crews near you.

What we're looking for

— Sign up the way a new user would: country, age, language picker.
— Plan a ride, invite a friend (or a second device), watch the RSVPs
  change through the week.
— Try the chat on a ride you organise and on one you've joined.
— Browse Discover and join a public ride.
— Anything that crashes, anything that's confusing, anything that
  reads weirdly in your language. Pelori is localised in English,
  Dutch, French, Spanish, and Italian — wrong-sounding translations
  are exactly the feedback we want.

What's not in this beta yet

Voice chat and the live group map are coming with a Premium tier
later this year. The buttons aren't in this build.

Sending feedback

Use the in-app form (Profile → Send feedback) or hit reply on any
TestFlight email. We read every message.

Thanks for riding with us. — The Pelori team

Pelori is the cycling app for group rides. It handles the four
things you message friends about before every ride: plan it, see
who's coming, keep the chat in one place, and find new rides
nearby.

PLAN A RIDE
Date, time, meeting pin, recurring or one-off. "Every Tuesday at
7pm" is one tap. Invite your crew and the ride lives somewhere
everyone can find. No more screenshotting WhatsApp polls.

RSVP PER OCCURRENCE
Going, not going, or maybe — answered per week. Tap the count to
see who's coming. Resets every Sunday so the regulars don't have
to rebook the same ride.

ONE THREAD PER RIDE
Group chat sits right next to the ride it belongs to. Replaces
five overlapping group chats with one conversation organised the
same way the rides are.

DISCOVER
Public rides from cycling clubs, bike shops, and weekend crews
near you. Browse the route and meeting point before you commit —
one tap to join.

BUILT FOR CREWS OF RIDERS
Whether you ride with a club of fifty or three friends, Pelori
scales without forcing you into a single workflow. Recurring shop
rides, ad-hoc Saturday loops, club race-prep sessions — all live
in the same simple "rides" model.

LOCALISED
Pelori speaks English, Dutch, French, Spanish, and Italian. Set
your account language and the whole app rebuilds.

PRIVACY AND YOUR DATA
Pelori does not sell your data. Coordinates aren't persisted
server-side. Profile pictures sit in private storage with signed
URLs. We use HTTPS everywhere and rotate credentials regularly.
Read the full policy at pelori.fit/privacy.

WHAT'S COMING
Voice chat and live group map are in development as part of an
upcoming Premium tier — talk to the crew and see who's where on
the route, hands-free. The standard tier is free and stays free.

Riding solo? Pelori isn't for you yet — try Strava. Pelori is for
riders who roll out together.

— The Pelori team
