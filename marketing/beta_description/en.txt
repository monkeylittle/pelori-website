Pelori — the bits a group ride actually needs

Pelori is a group-cycling app that handles the four things you
message friends about before every ride: plan it, see who's coming,
keep the chat in one place, and find new rides nearby.

This is the open beta. It's the standard tier — everything you need
to plan and run a recurring group ride:

— Plan a ride. Date, time, meeting pin, recurring or one-off. "Every
  Tuesday at 7pm" is one tap. Invite your crew and the ride lives
  somewhere everyone can find.
— RSVP per occurrence. Going, not going, or maybe. Resets every week
  so the regulars don't have to rebook the same ride.
— One thread per ride. Group chat right next to the ride it belongs
  to. Replaces the five overlapping group chats you've got now.
— Discover. Public rides from cycling clubs, bike shops, and weekend
  crews near you.

What we're looking for

— Sign up the way a new user would: country, age, language picker.
— Plan a ride, invite a friend (or a second device), watch the RSVPs
  change through the week.
— Try the chat on a ride you organise and on one you've joined.
— Browse Discover and join a public ride.
— Anything that crashes, anything that's confusing, anything that
  reads weirdly in your language. Pelori is localised in English,
  Dutch, French, Spanish, and Italian — wrong-sounding translations
  are exactly the feedback we want.

What's not in this beta yet

Voice chat and the live group map are coming with a Premium tier
later this year. The buttons aren't in this build.
