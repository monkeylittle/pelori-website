What it does

The bits a group ride actually needs.

No cockpit dashboards, no leaderboards, no "you're 0.4 km behind last Tuesday's split." Just the four things you message friends about before every ride.

P

Plan the ride

Date, time, meeting pin, recurring or one-off. "Every Tuesday at 7pm" is one tap. Invite your crew and the ride lives somewhere everyone can find.

R

Know who's coming

Per-occurrence RSVPs — going, not going, or maybe. Tap the count to see riders' faces. Resets every week so the regulars don't have to rebook the same ride.

C

One thread per ride

Group chat right next to the ride it belongs to. Replaces five overlapping group chats with the conversation organised the same way the rides are.

D

Find rides nearby

Public rides from cycling clubs, bike shops, and weekend crews. Browse the route and meeting point before you commit — one tap to join.
